My name is Natalia Campos and I am a daughter of immigrants from El Salvador and Colombia. Being Colombian, I grew up with alot of artistic and creative cultures and being Salvadorian, I grew up learning to fight and be strong. These two cultures made me who I am so I love drawing, painting, graphic design, doing MMA, doing construction, fixing cars, and playing all/any sports.
